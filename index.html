DODGE

Dodge is an American brand of automobiles and a division of Stellantis, based in Auburn Hills, Michigan.


The Dodge Charger is a model of automobile marketed by Dodge in various forms over seven generations since 1966. The first Charger was a show car in 1964.[1][2] A 1965 Charger II concept car resembled the 1966 production version
